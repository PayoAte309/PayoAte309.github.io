Everything you wanted to know about physical work, technique and tactics about goalkeepers...
My name is Pablo, and now I´m going to tell you a little bit about how did I like being a goalkeeper started to play soccer when I was 6. I started playing as a midfielder, unfortunatelly I wasn´t that good. so I leave soccer for a while, then I started playing volleyball untill I was 12, so I decided to return to soccer, but this time I was more skilled with the hands that with the feet. So I´ve been training as a goalkeeper for 4 years, and those who are missing...

Why I like it? Actually, I don´t know exactly how did I like it, but when I´m just under the goal, supporting, cheering and sometimes yelling at my team is a very exciting feeling, all your body active and your mind concentrated and pending to the slightest mistake. Incredibly it is very difficult to explain because you want so much to such an ungrateful position: where despite being the hero for 89 minutes, the slightest error makes you win the contempt of comrades, the fans, but the love of your rivals ... incredible, but true. Despite that, it is an affection that can not be explained and a joy when you make that shortcut that was impossible, and like a striker, you become a hero for an instant. Although once again I insist: a striker who fails a shot, has another attempt; But a goalkeeper who does not stop a ball, has no other attempt But once more; It is a love that is not explained, just feels and shows.
